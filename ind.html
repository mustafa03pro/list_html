Hyundai Creta Electric

The Hyundai Creta Electric is based on the facelifted model of the Creta that was launched in early 2024. The upcoming car gets EV-specific elements inside out to set itself apart from the ICE sibling. Rivals include the Maruti e Vitara, Tata Curvv EV, and the MG ZS EV. The Creta EV will be launched in India on 17 January at the 2025 Bharat Mobility Expo with bookings now open for Rs. 25,000.
